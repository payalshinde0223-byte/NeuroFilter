Understanding Internet Addiction

While the internet is a powerful tool, excessive use can lead to neurological changes similar to substance abuse. It affects the brain's reward system, specifically dopamine levels.

  • High usage is linked to sleep deprivation.
  • Social anxiety can often be a root cause.
  • Awareness is the first step toward digital detox.

"Digital health isn't about quitting the internet; it's about intentional usage that serves your life, not consumes it."
